About Super Bowl 1996

Super Bowl XXX was held on January 28, 1996, at Sun Devil Stadium in Tempe, Arizona. It featured a matchup between the Dallas Cowboys and the Pittsburgh Steelers.

Key Highlights

  • Larry Brown of the Dallas Cowboys was named the Super Bowl MVP.
  • The Cowboys defeated the Steelers with a score of 27-17.
  • The halftime show featured Diana Ross.
